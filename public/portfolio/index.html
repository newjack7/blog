Dating a medieval court roll

The previous post was spent learning the basics of text mining and exploring some possible avenues for analysis. In this post I label each offence with as precise a date as possible. In future I will also label it according to particular categories. I can do the first by extracting days, month, or years from the text to new columns and the pairing these with the original text. Essentially the text of the trials is broken into smaller observations and sorted according to particular criteria, in thie case, dates.
